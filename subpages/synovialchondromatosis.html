Notes

Orthobullets

Proliferative disease w/ cartilage metaplasia, 30-50yo most commonly, often knee. Late disease caught on x-ray, recommend MRI.

Findings: All of the presented images show large, well marginated erosions in the distal femur and proximal tibia. There is a prominent synovial proliferation with decreased signal on both T1 and T2 weighting extending throughout the spaces around the knee, including into the popliteus tendon sheath. T2 weighed image shows a moderately large joint effusion.
