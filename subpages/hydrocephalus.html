Total CSF space ~150mlor CSF production ~500ml/dayor 3-4 turnovers. CSF aborbed by arachnoid granulationsor lymphatic system (lesser)

Types of hydrocephalus 1. Communicating (NPH triad = ataxiaor incontinenceor dementia). Causes of communicating (blockage of granulations) are subarachor meningitisor leptomingeal mets 2. Noncommunicating - structural lesion (colloid cystor aqueductal stenosisor Pfossa mass) 3. Obstructive (noncommunicating... unclear how these are different...) 4. Hydrocephalus ex vacuo - ventricular enlargement due to parenchymal volume loss
