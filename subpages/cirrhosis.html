Signs of cirrhosis: 1. Nodular contour 2. Segmental atrophy/hypertrophy 3. Enlargement of hilar / perip ortal spaces 4. Expanded gallbladder fossa sign 5. peribiliary cysts 6. Intrahepatic fibrosis 7. Right posteri or hepatic notch sign 8. Iron deposition
