Main > Notes by disease > Hepatic Focal Nodular Hyperplasia

Notes

FNH is a hamartoma of disorganized liver tissue w/ no malignant potential. Very subtle on most sequences. Women>Menor no assn w/ contraceptives. Resembles background parenchyma (because it kind of is...) EXCEPT arterial phase imaging w/ large vascular supply. NO capsule. Most useful in the hepatobiliary phase (20 min delay).
